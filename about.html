My Journey

I am a junior developer, a hard worker, a father and a constant learner. My developer journey began officially in the spring of 2017 with my purchase of Colt Steele’s “The Web Developer bootcamp” followed shortly by “The Ultimate Web Designer and developer course" by Brad Hussey. Both of which I am still learning from every week. But my Developer learning was not limited to these two courses. I’ve spent time peeling off into the frameworks, languages and libraries that I've come across in those courses. This I believe has given me both an unorthodox and unique way of approaching web development. One in which my creativity can shine, goals are always set, hard work is expected and learning never ends.

I am a father to a little girl. By far my greatest joy in life. She never misses a chance to tell me that i talk to myself and make weird faces at the computer when Im coding.
I am a curious learner by heart. This is what drives and pushes me. No one thing can ever be learned too much. There is always another lesson on the horizon and I eagerly look forward to it.
